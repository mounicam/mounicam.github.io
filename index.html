About me

I am a Senior Language Modeling Engineer at Bloomberg AI working on adapting pretrained and language models for entity-centric analysis of news, social media, and financial documents. My research also focuses on building and evaluating controllable systems based on large language models for different text-generation tasks. I recently completed my Ph.D. in the School of Interactive Computing at Georgia Institute of Technology, advised by Professor Wei Xu. My dissertation focused on building and evaluating text simplifcation systems for different user groups and domains.

I received my Master's degree in Computer and Information Science from the University of Pennsylvania (UPENN) and my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering from the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT). Before joining my Ph.D., I also worked as a Software Development Engineer at Big Data Technologies, Amazon.
