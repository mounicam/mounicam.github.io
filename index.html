About me

I am a fifth year Ph.D. student in the School of Interactive Computing at Georgia Tech. My research interests are natural language generation, natural language processing, and social media. I am currently working on controllable text generation and text simplification with Professor Wei Xu as my advisor.

I received my Master's degree in Computer and Information Science from the University of Pennsylvania (UPENN) and my Bachelor's degree i n Computer Science and Engineering from the International Institute of Information Technology, Hyderabad (IIIT). Before joining my Ph.D., I worked as a Software Development Engineer at Big Data Technologies, Amazon.
